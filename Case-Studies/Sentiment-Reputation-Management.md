Case Study: Scaling Brand Reputation with AI
Project: Automated Sentiment Analysis & Response Engine
The Challenge: A boutique publishing house (or local service provider) was struggling with "Response Fatigue." They were receiving hundreds of social media comments and reviews across their titles. The owner couldn't distinguish between a simple "Thank you" and a critical customer service issue without reading every single one, leading to missed opportunities to fix negative experiences.

The "AutoPilot" Solution
I built a sentiment-aware gateway that sits between the customer and the business owner:

Sentiment Tiering: The script automatically labels incoming text as Positive, Neutral, or Critical.

Contextual Drafting: * Positive: Generates a unique "Thank You" to keep engagement high.

Critical: Flags the owner via notification and drafts an empathetic apology/solution for them to approve.

Relativity Brand Voice: The AI was prompted to ensure every response matched the professional and innovative tone of the "Relativity" brand.

The Results
Response Time: Improved from a 48-hour average to under 2 hours.

Escalation Accuracy: 100% of "Negative" reviews were flagged for human intervention immediately.

Customer Sentiment: Measured a 15% increase in repeat engagement due to the brand's new responsiveness.
