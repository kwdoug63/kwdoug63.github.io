Case Study: From Data Chaos to Automated Growth
Client: High-Volume Real Estate Consulting Firm
The Challenge: The client was receiving over 100 leads per week through various channels (social media, website, and referrals). Their data was "dirty"—names were in all caps, phone numbers were missing area codes, and 60% of the leads didn't have the budget for their services. The owner was spending 15+ hours a week manually vetting calls.

The "AutoPilot" Solution
I implemented a two-stage Python automation pipeline using the tools featured in my portfolio:

Stage 1: The Data Scrubber

Developed a script to standardize incoming lead data.

Fixed formatting errors and validated email addresses using Python’s re (Regular Expressions) and pandas libraries.

Stage 2: The AI Lead Qualifier

Integrated the OpenAI API to analyze the "Project Description" field of incoming inquiries.

The system scored leads from 1–10 based on the client's ideal budget and timeline.

The Results
Time Saved: Reduced the owner's manual vetting time from 15 hours to 2 hours per week.

Data Integrity: Achieved 100% standardization of CRM records, allowing for professional, personalized email marketing.

Revenue Impact: The client saw a 30% increase in conversion rates because they were able to respond to "High-Priority" leads within minutes instead of days.
